# Sample FAQ for Testing

This is a sample FAQ file for testing the Slack FAQ Bot with markdown source.

## How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:
1. Go to the Settings page
2. Click on "Security"
3. Select "Reset Password"
4. You'll receive an email with instructions to complete the reset

## What are the office hours?

Our office hours are:
- Monday-Friday: 9am-5pm PST
- Weekends: Closed

For urgent issues outside of office hours, please use our emergency contact line.

## Emergency contact

For critical issues that need immediate attention:
- Emergency Phone: 555-1234 (24/7)
- Email: emergency@example.com

Please only use this for true emergencies.

## How do I submit a support ticket?

To submit a support ticket:
1. Visit the support portal at support.example.com
2. Click "New Ticket"
3. Fill out the form with:
   - Your name and email
   - Issue category
   - Detailed description
   - Any relevant screenshots
4. Submit the form

You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket number. Response time is typically 24-48 hours for normal priority tickets.

## What is the return policy?

Our return policy is:
- 30-day return window from date of purchase
- Items must be in original condition with tags attached
- Proof of purchase required
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

Exceptions:
- Sale items are final sale
- Custom orders cannot be returned
- Opened software or digital products are non-refundable

## How do I update my billing information?

To update your billing information:
1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to "Account Settings"
3. Click on "Billing & Payment"
4. Select "Update Payment Method"
5. Enter your new payment details
6. Click "Save Changes"

Changes take effect immediately for future charges.
